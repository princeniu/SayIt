## App Information
- **Name**: SayIt - AI Voice to Text
- **Subtitle**: Minimalist Local Transcription
- **Category**: Productivity / Utilities

## Description (English)
SayIt is a lightweight, privacy-focused macOS application that transcribes your voice into text instantly.

**Key Features:**
- **Local AI Transcription**: Powered by OpenAI's Whisper, all processing happens on your device. Zero cloud, 100% private.
- **Micro-Sized Efficiency**: Optimized to stay out of your way in the menu bar.
- **Intelligent Audio Routing**: Automatically detects your iPhone microphone via Continuity—perfect for "Clamshell Mode".
- **Global Hotkey**: Transcribe from any app with a simple `⌥ Z`.
- **Seamless Workflow**: Automatically copies your text to the clipboard.

SayIt is built for writers, developers, and anyone who wants to capture thoughts at the speed of sound without compromising privacy.
